MFRS 119: Employee Benefits

An interactive guide to mastering recognition, measurement, and disclosure for accounting exams.

Short-Term Benefits

  • Payable within 12 months after the end of the period.
  • Wages, salaries, social security, paid annual leave.
  • Accounting: Recognise as an expense (unless capitalized) and a liability (accrued).

Post-Employment Benefits

Defined Contribution (DC)

Entity pays fixed contributions. Risk lies with employee. Easy accounting!

Defined Benefit (DB)

Entity's obligation is to provide agreed benefits. Actuarial/Investment risk lies with entity.

💡 Exam Tip: Remeasurements

Under MFRS 119, Remeasurements (actuarial gains/losses) for DB plans must be recognised in Other Comprehensive Income (OCI) and never reclassified to P&L!
